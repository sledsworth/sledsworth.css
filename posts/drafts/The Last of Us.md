A quick rant at the top: Games are too damn long. I thought Last of Us Part I was a touch on the long side—Part II is far longer. I thought I was near the end at the midway point.

I couldn't help but feel totally disconnected from the woman Ellie became. No, I don't mean her love interest, I'm talking about her relentless killing to avenge Joel. I don't buy that. That isn't the Ellie from the first game. That isn't the Ellie Joel helped realize.
