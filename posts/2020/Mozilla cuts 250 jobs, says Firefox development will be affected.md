[MDN](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/) is arguably the most valuable web developer resource around—and its future now is in jeopardy. As an individual making my living developing for the web, I find this unsettling. Even more, I hadn't a clue that [Google was responsible for the vast majority of Mozilla's revenue](https://www.zdnet.com/article/sources-mozilla-expected-to-extend-its-google-search-deal/).

We are dangerously close to a world where Google has sole ownership of the web and the portal to access it.
