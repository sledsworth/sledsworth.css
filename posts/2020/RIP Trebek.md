My family wasn't a Jeopardy household growing up, so I don't have the same kind of fond memories many people have for Trebek. I have seen Jeopardy bring people together in a way that was consistent and wholesome—especially at events around the holidays. No one could *dislike* the show, and I think that is all thanks to Trebek.

Rest in peace Alex. ❤️
