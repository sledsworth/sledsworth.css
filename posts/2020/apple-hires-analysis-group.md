Apple seems to have missed the point of the 30% argument—it’s not that the other tech giants are paying platform developers better, it’s that 30% is just too damn much across the board. The difference is that we have a certain expectation from Apple to lead the way in this change. 

Also, the other platforms don’t have the same strict policy Apple’s AppStore has. If you want to spin up your own payment platform and have users sign up directly in the Android app you can, effectively eliminating the 30% cut Google would take.

Finally, we are talking about comparing the 4-5 largest companies in the world. If anyone can spare some change, it’d be them.
