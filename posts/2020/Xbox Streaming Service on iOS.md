No, not [xCloud](https://www.xbox.com/en-US/xbox-game-pass/cloud-gaming/home), the other service—called *Xbox Game Streaming*. Given AppStore rule [4.2.7 Remote Desktop Clients](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/review/guidelines/#minimum-functionality), it would seem just streaming the Xbox console content is allowed. In fact, [Sony's Remote Play App](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ps4-remote-play/id1436192460) is already doing this. 

I'm not sure what's worse—[Xbox's confusingly similar name for all of its endeavors](https://www.polygon.com/2020/9/22/21451484/xbox-series-x-preorder-one-mistake-s-microsoft-naming), or Apple's user hostile rules and guidelines.

Ehh...it's Apple's hostile rules and guidelines.
